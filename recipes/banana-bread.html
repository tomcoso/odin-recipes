Banana Bread

Ingredients

  • 1 3/4 cups (210 g) spelt flour (whole wheat, unbleached all-purpose, or gluten free blend)
  • 1/3 cup (75 g) organic pure cane sugar (or 1/2 finely chopped dates)
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • pinch of mineral salt
  • 1/3 cup (75 ml) neutral flavored oil (or coconut oil in liquid state, vegan butter at room temp or applesauce
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 4 small or 3 large overripe bananas (about 1 1/2 - 1 3/4 cups (338-410g)), mashed
  • 1/4 cup (56 ml) almond milk, use only if needed

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F. Grease your loaf pan.
  2. In a medium/large size bowl, combine flour, sugar, baking powder, baking soda and salt, set aside.
  3. In a medium bowl, mash bananas. Add the oil, vanilla and bananas to the dry ingredients and mix until combined, do not overmix. If mixture seems too thick, add the almond milk
  4. Pour batter into a greased loaf pan.
  5. Bake for about 50 min - 1 hour.
  6. Remove from oven and let cool for 10 min before slicing.
